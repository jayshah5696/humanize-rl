## Key Insights

1. **Humanization works** — Gemini 3.1 Pro successfully removes AI patterns.
   Mean humanized score (0.907) exceeds original human (0.865).

2. **Over-recovery** — 112.9% recovery ratio means the humanizer
   over-corrects. It produces text that is "more human-scoring" than actual
   human text. This makes sense: real humans use some em-dashes, some
   hedging, some structure. The humanizer removes ALL of them.

3. **Layer 1 ceiling found** — AUROC human-vs-humanized is 0.31 (below
   chance). This means Layer 1 alone cannot detect well-humanized text.
   This is the exact use case for Layer 2 (LLM judge).

4. **100% SFT yield** — All 50 triples produce valid training pairs.
   At scale with quality filters this will drop, but the pipeline works.

5. **JSON parsing issues** — Gemini 3.1 Pro initially failed on structured
   output with `max_tokens: 1024` (reasoning tokens consumed the budget).
   Fixed by bumping to `max_tokens: 4096`.
